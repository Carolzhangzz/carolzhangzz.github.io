Hi, I'm Carol Zhang. I'm currently a Computer Science Master student at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD).

My main research interests focus on AI for Social Good and Human-AI Interaction. I have designed and developed conversational agents leveraging NLP techniques to evaluate and intervene user's environmental attitudes and behaviors.

I have worked with Prof. Steven Dow at UCSD Design Lab. I also have worked with Dr.Ray LC at HK CityU, resulting in a co-first authored paper published at CHI'24 and a first authored paper at CHI'25.

I am happy to collaborate or just chat about research, my email is qiz065@ucsd.edu.
