Qinshi (Carol) Zhang



Hi, I'm Carol Zhang. I'm a CS master student at UCSD.


My research interests focus on Human-AI Interaction for Social Impact and AI for Social Good, particularly the societal implications of technology. This includes designing chatbots and integrating multimodal agents in games to promote sustainability awareness among players. Recently, I have also explored Al for productivity tools.


I'm working with Prof. Steven Dow at UCSD Design Lab. I also have worked with Dr.Ray LC at HK CityU on AI for Social Good, resulting in a co-first authored paper published at CHI 2024.
